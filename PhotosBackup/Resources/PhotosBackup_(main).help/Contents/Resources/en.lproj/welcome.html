Introduction

Photos Backup is a small helper application that manages and automates the backing up of an Apple Photos library to another location. Features:

  • Most filesystems can be used as a backup target as Photos Backup automatically creates a sparse bundle disk image
  • Uses the fast and efficient rsync utility to archive files, saving time and energy
  • Backs up to remote network volumes
  • Schedule backups for hourly, weekly or monthly repeats
